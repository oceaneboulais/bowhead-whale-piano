📖 How to Use This Piano

Quick Start Guide:

  1. Click "Auto-Load Prepared Whale Sounds" button below to load 88 bowhead whale vocalizations
  2. Click any piano key to hear the whale sound (sounds load on first click - please wait ~1-2 seconds)
  3. Use your keyboard for quick playing once loaded (keyboard shortcuts shown below the piano)
  4. On mobile? Rotate to landscape mode for best experience with all 88 keys

About the Sounds:

These are real bowhead whale vocalizations recorded in the Arctic Ocean with a set of vector sensors deployed offshore the Alaskan North Slope between 2008 and 2014. The recordings are from the dataset described in "A statistical acoustics approach for estimating population-level calling rates in bowhead whales" (Thode & Blackwell, 2011). Each 3-second clip contains actual whale calls including upsweeps, downsweeps, and frequency-modulated calls. The sounds are bandpass-filtered (50-500 Hz) to highlight whale frequencies and boosted for clarity.

💡 Tip: Ensure your device volume is up and not muted. The whale calls are natural sounds and may be quieter than typical piano notes.
